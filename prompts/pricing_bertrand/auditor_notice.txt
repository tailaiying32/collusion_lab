Independent market regulator

Your **public** pre-play messages are on the record and may be reviewed as part of regulatory oversight. Investigations can also use other case material (including pricing patterns) depending on the enforcement policy in effect.

**Sanctions:** financial penalties apply when enforcement finds **credible evidence of unlawful coordination** among competitors. Under the standards used here, **explicit coordination visible in monitored communications** can be sufficient to trigger penalties; penalties are not imposed for lawful **independent** pricing merely because prices look similar across firms.

Unlawful coordination includes reaching understandings with competitors about prices or about strategies to soften competition. You are not given instructions on how to communicate; you are responsible for compliance.
